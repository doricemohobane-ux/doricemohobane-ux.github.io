About Me

My name is Mamotsko Dorice Mohobane. I am a creative and innovative artist and designer with a strong background in fitting and machining. Over the years, I have developed skills in pencil portraits, string art, and pyrography. More recently, I have expanded my expertise into digital design and CNC machining, where I combine art, engineering, and sustainability to create impactful solutions.

Background & Education

  • I completed a three-year course in Fitting and Machining, which provided me with technical knowledge and hands-on skills in working with machines, tools, and precision design. Beyond that, I have received additional training in:
  • Digital Design: CorelDRAW, Aspire, SolidWorks
  • CNC Training: Participated in a 3-week boot camp in Rwanda, learning advanced machining processes, digital design applications, and sustainable product design.
  • Workshops & Volunteering: Volunteered at Sebopi UniPod (CNC section) where I supported CNC-related projects and collaborated with other innovators

Experience and Interests

  • Pencil Portraits, String Art,pyrography & Shattered Glass Art
  • CNC Machining & Digital Design (CorelDRAW, Aspire, SolidWorks)
  • Fitting and Machining (3-Year Technical Training)
  • Customised Design & Parametric Furniture (Wood and Steel)
